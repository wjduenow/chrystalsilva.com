More about me, and my life.

I am originally from Sri Lanka, I arrived here in December 1999, starting the New Year in New York city. The Bay Area was where my mom and my sister moved settled. My father was still living in Sri Lanka because he was a Colonel at the Sri Lankan army fighting a civil war for what seemed like an eternity. I would visit my father during my summer holidays and I could not believe how different the two worlds I was living in were. I was living in the Bay Area with everything I could ask for and more. My father finally re-joined our family after five almost six years apart and the war was finished two years later in 2008.

Going back to Sri Lanka every few years, I have seen my birth country growing and changing. My father has always encouraged us to be sustainable, recycle and not to waste (even food). This last semester in school has really helped me focus and take action on something that I have been passionate about, and that is finding solutions to the waste problems we face in our community day to day.
